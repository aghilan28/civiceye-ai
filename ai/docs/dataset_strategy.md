# Dataset Strategy

CivicEye Phase A targets pothole detection only.

## Required Data Balance

The dataset must include both positive and negative images.

Positive examples:

- potholes
- broken road cavities
- severe road surface damage

Negative examples:

- clean roads
- shadows
- puddles
- lane markings
- oil stains
- manhole covers
- non-damaged rough roads

## Coverage Requirements

Collect across:

- daylight
- low light
- wet roads
- rain
- different asphalt/concrete textures
- mobile phone angles
- dashcam angles
- near and far distances
- compression artifacts
- motion blur

## Source Policy

Every source must track license, acquisition date, annotation format, source ID, and whether images can be used for commercial model training.
